# Age Verification Policy

**Last updated:** [DATE]

Grid includes explicit adult content. To comply with the UK Online Safety Act 2023 (enforced by Ofcom) and the German Interstate Treaty on the Protection of Minors in the Media (JMStV), we require every user to complete age verification before accessing or uploading explicit content.

## How It Works

1. At signup, and again before your first view or upload of explicit content, you're asked to verify you're 18+.
2. This age check is handled by our accredited third-party provider, [PROVIDER NAME], using one of: facial age estimation, ID document check, open banking check, or a recognized digital identity wallet. We use an accredited provider for this specific check — not a manual review by our team — because both the UK and German legal frameworks require it.
3. We receive only a **pass/fail result and a reference token** from the provider — we never receive or store your ID document, your photo, or any biometric template. The provider processes and discards that data per their own retention policy, which we require to be minimal and compliant with GDPR.
4. If verification fails or is inconclusive, you can retry with a different method, or contact support.
5. Verification does not expire immediately, but we periodically require re-verification per our internal risk policy.

Separately, our optional **Photo Verification** badge — which confirms your profile photo matches a live selfie, as a community-trust signal rather than an age check — may be reviewed by our own team at low volume, or by automated matching as we scale. This is a different, non-legally-mandated process from the age check described above.

## Why We Do This

Self-declared age ("tick here to confirm you're 18") is explicitly rejected as insufficient under the UK Online Safety Act, and Germany's JMStV has required closed-user-group verification for adult content for over a decade. Rather than build two separate compliance paths, Grid applies one age-assurance standard globally, meeting the stricter of the applicable requirements everywhere we operate.

## Your Rights

Verification is mandatory to access explicit content but does not cost you anything, is never used for advertising, and the resulting data is limited to the pass/fail token described above. You can request details of what verification data we hold about you via **Profile → Data & Privacy**.

# Cookie Policy

**Last updated:** [DATE]

## What Cookies We Use

| Category | Purpose | Set by default? |
|---|---|---|
| **Essential** | Login sessions, security (CSRF protection), load balancing | Yes — required for the app to function, cannot be disabled |
| **Functional** | Remembering your language, PWA install prompts, notification preferences | Yes, but adjustable |
| **Analytics** | Understanding feature usage to improve the app (aggregated, no ad profiles built) | **No — opt-in only** |
| **Marketing** | We currently do not run advertising and do not use marketing cookies | N/A |

## Your Choices

When you first visit Grid, you'll see a cookie preference banner defaulting to **essential-only**. You can change your choices at any time from **Profile → Privacy & Anonymity Controls → Cookie Preferences**, or via the cookie icon in the footer on desktop web.

## Why Essential-Only by Default

Under the ePrivacy Directive (as implemented in the UK and Germany) and GDPR, only strictly necessary cookies may be set without consent. Everything else — including analytics — requires your affirmative opt-in, which is how we've built this from day one.
